Jedi

Who are the Jedi?

The Jedi are selected members of society who know how to access and use the force.
Together, or alone, they fight against the evil within the galaxy. Their prime weapon is the lightsaber,
but many use the force as a tool as well.

Rey:

Her parents seemingly abandoned her on Jakku at a young age.
She grew up as a scavenger, scrounging up whatever food she could manage.
When she agreed to help a droid on an urgent mission from the resistance, her life changed forever.
Rey discovered the force was strong with her, and she sought out training from the vanished Luke Skywalker.
Through resiliance and grit, Rey continued to train. Her connection to Kylo Ren soon became known.
She then faced her greatest test: a confrontation with fear and evil that would soon determine the galaxy's fate.

Luke Skywalker:

Luke Skywalker started off as a Tatooine farmboy.
With his friends Princess Leia and Han Solo, Luke batteled the evil Empire.
On his journy he discovered the truth about his lineage, ended the tyranny of the Sith, and soon became
the greatest Jedi the galaxy had ever seen. A generation later, he went into hiding,
the location of the former Jedi master was undisclosed.
He was haunted by Ben Solo's failed teachings and fall to evil. However, his
solitude was soon interrupted.
Luke Skywalker now had one final, momentous role in the fight between good and evil.

Yoda:

Yoda was a legendary Jedi Master and his connection to the force was never before seen.
Although he was small in stature, he was extremely wise and powerful.
He trained Jedi for over 800 years, and played integral roles in the Clone Wars,
the training of Luke Skywalker, and unlocking the path to immorality.
