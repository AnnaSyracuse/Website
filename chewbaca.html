Who is Chewbaca?

Chewbaca, a legendary Wookiee warrior! He is known for working with his longtime copiolot: Han Solo.
Together, he and Han heloped to carry out dangerous missions agains the First Order.
He was known as Chewie to his closest friends and was a part of the core group that helped return freedom to the galaxy!
He is known for a short temper, but extremely accurate on the bowcaster. He has a huge heart and will do anything for his friends.
Maybe you might catch a glimpse of the Millennium Falcon as it races towards its next adventure!
