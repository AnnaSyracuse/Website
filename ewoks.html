Ewoks

Who are the Ewoks?

The Ewoks are native to the moon of Endor. They are curious animals that are around one meter tall.
Their skills include forest survival and the building of primitive technology like gliders and catapults.
They are quick learners when it comes to new and advanced technology.
The Ewoks accepted members of the Rebel Alliance into their tribe, and allied themselves to their cause.
The Ewoks helped to destroy the Imperial shield generator built in their forests, and their primitive weapons
helped to eliminate the stormtroopers and the scout walkers of the Empire.
Their help allowed the essential victory at the Battle of Endor.
